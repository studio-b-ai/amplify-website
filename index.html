01 — How It Works

Four layers. One approval gate.

Amplify is a pipeline, not a point tool. Each layer has one job.

L1
Discover
Six LinkedIn signals surface high-intent prospects. Everything saved to S3 CSV, timestamped and signed.
L2
Extract
Post-Commenter and Liker phantoms extract enriched contact data. Results pushed to webhook-router, HMAC-signed.
L3
Score + Route
Claude scores ICP fit, drafts in your voice, and posts to Slack with Approve / Edit / Skip. No publish without approval.
L4
Execute
Approved actions fire through LinkedIn API as your authenticated identity. Every action writes to the HMAC audit ledger.
