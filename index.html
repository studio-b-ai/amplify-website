§B — How it works

Four layers. One approval gate.

Amplify is a pipeline, not a point tool. Each layer has one job. Most operators stitch five tools together. This is the one workflow.

L1
Discover
Six LinkedIn signals surface high-intent prospects. Everything saved to S3 CSV, timestamped and signed.
L2
Extract
Post-Commenter and Liker phantoms extract enriched contact data. Results pushed to webhook-router, HMAC-signed.
L3
Score + Route
Claude scores ICP fit, drafts in your voice, and posts to Slack with Approve / Edit / Skip. No publish without approval.
L4
Execute
Approved actions fire through LinkedIn API as your authenticated identity. Every action writes to the HMAC audit ledger.

§E — Compliance

Your account. Your data. Your audit trail.

Three exhibits buyers read in order: is my LinkedIn at risk, what can Amplify see, and what does the agency control surface look like.

§E.1 — LinkedIn safety
Your account is not at risk.

Amplify is scheduled via Slack and published through authenticated LinkedIn API access (when approved — see methodology¹). Never a scraped session, never a stolen cookie, never a headless browser logged in as you.

Your account is not at risk because we don't touch it in a way LinkedIn punishes.

§E.2 — Privacy
What Amplify sees, and what it doesn't.

Sees: public LinkedIn signals (profile, posts, engagement), drafts you generate through the Slack app, metadata your CRM already holds.

Does not see: your DMs, your saved items, your connections' private profiles, any email you haven't explicitly connected.
